KIE'S COFFEE

When we started out in ‘09 back in Jakarta, Indonesia. We roasted coffee for ourselves, pretty much because we were some of the few people around to drink it. Several years later, our focus has grown a little broader, that is we roasting delicious coffee and creating meaningful coffee experiences for folks who simply love coffee

be they café owners or enthusiastic home brewers - coffee drinkers! And luckily, we still get to drink it too. Perhaps our core product is not in fact coffee, but instead, what happens over a coffee shared-generous hospitality.
